Investigation Overview¶

Make people understand the relationship between the rating of the loan and the character of the borrower. With a understanding on the type of loan the platform given out.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset is a data set of a loan request made on the prosper platform. The detail of the loan are describe in the dataset such as how much it was borrowed, the rate that the borrower pay, the status and time of the payment. The dataset mainly focuses on the characteristic of each loan that were requested in the platform.

Rating and yield¶

We can observe from the data that the higher the rating the lower the rate for borrower and the higher the loan amount given. This seems to be a logical relationship since the higher rating should have lower default probability thus higher amount given.

Rating, Default¶

We could see that the higher the rating the lower the default probabilitly. But this came with exception of the A Grade which has higher default probability than B and C grade. The reason that might be behind this is that higher proportion of loan are given at A grade, although this default probability has been in % of total loan given per grades. But it still haven't eliminate this effect. Showing that the A grade really have higher default probability
